================================
E-COMMERCE STORE POLICIES
================================

1. GENERAL INFORMATION
----------------------
Welcome to our online shop. By accessing or purchasing from our store, you agree to be bound by the following policies.
These policies are designed to ensure transparency, fairness, and trust between our business and our customers.

2. ORDERING POLICY
------------------
- All orders must be placed through our official website or mobile app.
- Customers are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of order details (product, size, quantity, shipping address).
- Orders are confirmed only after successful payment authorization.
- We reserve the right to cancel or refuse orders suspected of fraud or abuse.

3. PAYMENT POLICY
-----------------
- Accepted payment methods: Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and authorized BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later) providers.
- Payments must be completed at checkout. No cash on delivery is accepted unless explicitly offered.
- Refunds are processed back to the original payment method only.
- Prices are displayed in the selected currency; final charges may include applicable taxes and duties.

4. SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
-----------------------------
- We ship orders worldwide, subject to carrier availability and restrictions.
- Standard processing time: 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).
- Delivery estimates depend on carrier and location; delays due to customs or unforeseen events are not our responsibility.
- Customers will receive tracking information once orders are shipped.
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the order was canceled before dispatch.

5. RETURNS & REFUNDS POLICY
---------------------------
- Customers may request a return within 30 days of delivery.
- Returned items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags intact.
- Certain items (e.g., underwear, personal care, customized products) are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
- Refunds will be issued within 7–14 business days of return approval and inspection.
- Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product was defective or incorrect.

6. EXCHANGE POLICY
------------------
- Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
- Customers must initiate an exchange request within 30 days of delivery.
- If the replacement item is not available, customers may opt for store credit or a full refund (if applicable).

7. WARRANTY POLICY
------------------
- Selected products are covered under a limited warranty as specified on the product page.
- Warranties cover manufacturing defects but do not apply to damage caused by misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.
- Warranty claims must be supported by proof of purchase and photographs of the defect.

8. PRIVACY & DATA POLICY
------------------------
- We value customer privacy and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.
- Personal data collected during orders (name, address, payment details) is used solely for order fulfillment and communication.
- We never sell or rent customer data to third parties.
- Customers may request deletion of their data in accordance with legal requirements.

9. SECURITY POLICY
------------------
- All transactions are secured using SSL encryption and industry-standard payment gateways.
- Customers are responsible for safeguarding their login credentials.
- We will never request sensitive information (passwords, credit card PINs) via email or phone.

10. CANCELLATION POLICY
-----------------------
- Orders may be canceled before shipment. Once dispatched, cancellations are not possible.
- In case of cancellation, refunds will be issued back to the original payment method.

11. PROMOTIONS & DISCOUNTS POLICY
---------------------------------
- Discounts, vouchers, and promotional codes are valid for a limited time as specified.
- Multiple discounts may not be combined unless explicitly stated.
- Promotions are not retroactive and cannot be applied to previous purchases.

12. CUSTOMER SERVICE POLICY
---------------------------
- Customer support is available via email, live chat, or phone during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9:00–18:00 local time).
- Response times may vary but we aim to resolve all queries within 48 hours.
- Escalations are handled by a dedicated customer experience manager.

13. COMPLIANCE & LEGAL POLICY
-----------------------------
- Our business complies with international consumer protection laws and fair trade standards.
- Disputes will be subject to the governing law and jurisdiction of our registered business location.

14. POLICY UPDATES
------------------
- We reserve the right to update these policies at any time without prior notice.
- The latest version will always be published on our website.
